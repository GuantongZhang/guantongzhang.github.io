About Us

“With expertise, passion, and care, we turn your Southern California dream homes into reality.”

Jie Zheng

CA DRE #01969518

As a top real estate agent with extensive experience, Jie is known for her focus on delivering positive results and her strong expertise in real estate market conditions. She is a member of the President’s Circle and was ranked as the #2 National Performing Agent in sales in 2018. Jie earned a Master’s degree from Lehigh University with a concentration in Business Data Analysis.
Having immigrated from China in 2004, Jie speaks fluent Mandarin and has a deep understanding of Chinese culture, enabling her to effectively serve a broad client base both locally and internationally.
Jie currently lives in Irvine with her husband and two children. Whether you are looking to buy or sell a home, she would be honored to meet with you and discuss your real estate needs.

Maggie Pang

CA DRE #02186012

With 16 years of real estate investment experience, Maggie brings a practical, investor-minded approach to every transaction. She has flipped over 30 properties across Southern California, giving her clients an edge when it comes to pricing, negotiations, and spotting opportunities.
Maggie is passionate about real estate investing and remodeling, with a keen eye for interior and landscape design. She enjoys helping clients see possibilities beyond what is on the surface and takes pride in making the process smooth and rewarding, while guiding them through one of the most important financial decisions of their lives.
Fluent in Chinese, Maggie proudly serves a diverse client base. She is based in Orange County, where she lives with her husband, two children, and a very beloved dog. When she is not working, you’ll likely find her out running, swimming, training for relay races.
